The Kripke Era is the era of seasons helmed by the show's creator Eric Kripke and co-showrunner Robert Singer. The era consists of season 1 up to 5.

Season 1

"Dad's on a hunting trip, and he hasn't been home in a few days"
- Dean to Sam

The first Supernatural season has 22 episodes. It premiered on September 13 2005 with the episode Pilot and ended May 4 2006 with the episode Devil's Trap.

Summary

After their mother's death in a fire when they're just children, Sam and Dean Winchester, along with their father, John, spend their life hunting the demon that killed her: "The Yellow-Eyed Demon". All the while also saving people from random monsters. That's until Sam decides he's had enough and runs off to law school, leaving Dean with their dad.

> In the first episode we see Sam at law school, but then Dean breaks into the dorm he shares with his girlfriend Jessica to inform him their dad's gone missing on a hunt and asking Sam for help. Sam goes, but after this he'll go back to school and stop hunting definetly. When they get back from finishing the hunt their dad started though, Sam goes inside his dorm, and immediatly sees Jessica on the ceiling, blood dripping from her body, the appartment catches fire. Jessica dies the same way Sam and Dean's mother died all those years ago. Sam goes back to Dean and they begin their search for their father.

Sam and Dean hunt monsters such as vampires, Bloody Mary, ghosts, while searching for their father. They discover Sam is a psychic and there are more people like him with special abilities, who all were visited by the Yellow-Eyed Demon exactly when they were 6 months old and fed his blood. Apparantly he means for them to become generals in his army. They reunite with their father, who has found a way to kill the Yellow-Eyed Demon: the Colt, a gun that can kill anything. But someone else is after the gun too: Meg, a demon that works for the Yellow-Eyed Demon.

In the last episode Meg has kidnapped John. They lure Meg into Bobby Singer's, an old friend, house and exorcise Meg. They go to rescue John but the Yellow-Eyed Demon appears. They fight and the demon flees The season ends with the Winchesters getting hit by a truck.

Season 2

That's crap. You always have a choice. You can either roll over and die or you can keep fighting, no matter what.
- Dean to reaper

The second season of supernatural has 22 epsiodes. It's premiere was September 28 2006 with In My Time of Dying and it ended May 17 2007 with All Hell Breaks Loose - Part 2.

Summary

In the first episode Sam, Dean and John are in the hospital after last season's car crash. Dean is the worst off, he walks around the hospital hovering between life and death. John summons the Yellow-Eyed Demon and makes a deal: the Colt and his own life for Dean's. The demon takes the deal, Dean is saved and John is dead. Before John dies, he tells Dean that if he can't save Sam from becoming evil he will have to kill him.

Through a voicemail for their dad the boys meet Ellen and her daughter Jo, who run a roadhouse specifically for hunters. They hunt a lot of monsters, including ghosts, a djinn and demons.

Sam gets kidnapped by the Yellow-Eyed Demon and gets put in a town with the other special children, they learn the demon's endgame is an all-out war against the human race. After a few of them die, Sam learns it was another special child that tells him only one of them can survive. Eventually it's down to two, Sam and another kid, Jake. Just as Dean finds Sam, he gets stabbed in the spine and dies.

In the finale Dean can't accept his brother's death so he makes a deal with a crossroad's demon, Dean gets one more year to live in exchange for his brother's life. Sam comes back. They discover the Yellow-Eyed Demon wants to open the Devil's Gate, a crypt that opens a gate to hell, but demons can't get in because it's in the middle of a giant Devil's Trap made up of train rails. That's what he needs Jake for, to open the crypt. They meet the demon at the Gate with Bobby and Ellen, but they're too late. The crypt is open and demons and spirits are pouring out. Then Dean sees the Colt that the demon gave to Jake and grabs it, the demon disarms him. But then the demon is grabbed by John Winchester's ghost, John gets overpowered easily, but the distraction is what Dean needs to grab the colt and kill the demon. They close the Devil's Gate and John ascends into heaven.

Season 3

We're all going to Hell, Dean. Might as well enjoy the ride.
- Bela to Dean

The third season of Supernatural has 16 episodes. It premiered on October 4 2007 with The Magnificent Seven and ended on May 15 2008 with No Rest For The Wicked.

Summary

Hundreds of demons have been released from hell, and Sam and Dean are set on sending as much of them back while also trying to find a way to break Dean's deal that he made saving Sam that made it so he'd die in a year.

They meet Ruby, who saves them from a few demons, at first she says she's a hunter, she knows about Azazel (The Yellow-Eyed Demon), and Sam confronts her, asking how she knows so much, she reveals she's actually a demon. Sam wants to fight her, but stops when she promises to help break Dean's deal.

There's also Bela, an antagonist that knows about the supernatural but uses it to make money by scamming and stealing instead of saving people. She clashes with the Winchesters several times. She steals the Colt, and Dean goes to get it back, but he discovers she bartered it off to Lilith, a high-ranking demon, in an attempt to break the deal she made to kill her abusive father. Bela's times is almost up and she begs Dean to help her, but he refuses and she gets ripped to shreds by hellhounds.

The boys discover that Lilith holds all hell contracts so they go to kill her, together with Ruby. Ruby says Sam has to use his psychic powers but Dean refuses. Lilith is currently possesing a little girl and terrorising her family. But when they finally corner her, Lilith is gone. Dean accepts he is going to die, but they still lock up inside a room and line the doors and windows with goofer dust, which keeps hellhounds out. Suddenly Ruby demands the demon-killing knife and they discover she's actually Lilith now, she holds Sam against the wall, who still begs for his brother, but she ignores him and lets the hellhounds in. They tear Dean to shreds. Lilith goes to kill Sam, but sees her power has no hold over him anymore, and flees. The last shot is of Dean in hell, strung up and calling Sam's name.

Season 4

The fourth season has 22 episodes. It premiered on September 18 2008 with Lazarus Rising and ended on May 14 2009 with Lucifer Rising.

Summary

The season starts with Dean literally crawling out of his grave. He reunites with Sam and Bobby, but they don't know who or what brought Dean back. After learning the creature's name: Castiel, Dean and Bobby decide to just summon it. Castiel comes, and he reveals he is an Angel of the Lord
