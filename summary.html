Season 1

"Dad's on a hunting trip, and he hasn't been home in a few days"

The first Supernatural season has 22 episodes. It premiered on September 13 2005 with the episode Pilot and ended May 4 2006 with the episode Devil's Trap.

Summary

After their mother's death in a fire when they're just children, Sam and Dean Winchester, along with their father, John, spend their life hunting the demon that killed her: "The Yelllow-Eyed Demon". All the while also saving people from random monsters. That's until Sam decides he's had enough and runs off to law school, leaving Dean with their dad.

> In the first episode we see Sam at law school, but then Dean breaks into the dorm he shares with his girlfriend Jessica to inform him their dad's gone missing on a hunt and asking Sam for help. Sam goes, but after this he'll go back to school and stop hunting definitly. When they get back from finishing the hunt their dad started though, Sam goes inside his dorm, and immediatly sees Jessica on the ceiling, blood dripping from her body, the appartment catches fire. Jessica dies the same way Sam and Dean's mother died all those years ago. Sam goes back to Dean and they begin their search for their father.

Sam and Dean hunt monsters such as vampires, Bloody Mary, ghosts, while searching for their father. They discover Sam is a psychic and there are more people like him with special abilities, who all were visited by the Yellow-Eyed Demon exactly when they were 6 months old. They reunite with their father, who has found a way to kill the Yellow-Eyed Demon: the Colt, a gun that can kill anything. But someone else is after the gun too: Meg, a demon that works for the Yellow-Eyed Demon.

In the last episode Meg has kidnapped John. They lure Meg into Bobby Singer's, an old friend, house and exorcise Meg. They go to rescue John but the Yellow-Eyed Demon appears. They fight and the demon flees The season ends with the Winchesters getting hit by a truck.
