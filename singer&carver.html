The Singer & Carver Era is the era of the show when Jeremy Carver and Robert Singer became co-showrunners after Sera Gamble left. It consists of Season 8 - Season 11

  • Season 8
  • Season 9
  • Season 10
  • Season 11

Season 8

The eighth season of Supernatural has 23 episodes. It premiered on October 3 2012 with We Need To Talk About Kevin and ended on May 15 2013 with Sacrifice.

Season 9

The ninth season of Supernatural has 23 episodes. It premiered on October 8 2013 with I Think I'm Gonna Like It Here and it ended on May 20 2014 with Do You Believe In Miracles?.

Season 10

The tenth season of Supernatural has 23 episodes. It premiered on October 7 2014 with Black and ended with Brother's Keeper

Season 11

The eleventh season of Supernatural has 23 episodes. It premiered on October 7 2015 with Out of the Darkness, Into the Fire and ended on May 25 2016 with Alpha and Omega
