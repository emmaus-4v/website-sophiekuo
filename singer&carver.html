Season 8

"Hey, listen, we'll figure it out, okay, just like we always do. Come on, let it go, okay. Let it go, little brother."
- Dean to Sam

The eighth season of Supernatural has 23 episodes. It premiered on October 3 2012 with We Need To Talk About Kevin and ended on May 15 2013 with Sacrifice.

Summary

After one year of being in Purgatory, Dean returns to Earth, without Castiel, carrying inside his arm the soul of a vampire named Benny. The Sam and Dean start a fight against Crowley to find the Demon Tablet and lock the Gates of Hell, so all demons stay in Hell forever. The brothers use Kevin Tran, the prophet andd only person that can read the Tablets of God, to help them read the tablet and close the gates.

Castiel is brought back from Purgatory by an angel named Naomi, who controls him and wants to use him to get the Angel Tablet and kill Dean, Castiel almost does this but Dean brings him back. While Kevin works on translating the tablet, Sam and Dean meet their grandfather, Henry Winchester, who was a Man of Letters, an organization dedicated to supernatural knowledge. He dissapeared in 1958, but this was actually him using a time-travel spell to go to the future and escape the demon Abaddon. Henry is killed protecting Sam and Dean, but he gives them access to the Men of Letters bunker, a bunker where there's many supernatural facts and books, which the brothers then take as a new homebase. Kevin translates that three trials must be done to lock the Gates of Hell forever, Sam completes the first two, but Dean ends the trials immediatley after he finds out they will kill Sam. Meanwhile, Castiel is tricked by an angel called Metatron into completing another series of trials that would have allegedly locked all angels in Heaven, so Castiel could fix things there, but actually banished every angel apart from Metatron to Earth, the season ended with the angels falling from the sky like meteorites, their wings burning off, and Castiel looking up at them, graceless.
