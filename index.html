About

Supernatural was created by Eric Kripke in 2005. The show follows brothers Sam and Dean Winchester as they investigate paranormal events and fight monsters around the United States.

Recently Supernatural ended it's 15 year run with the 20th episode of it's 15th season: Carry On.
