Season 6

The sixth season of Supernatural has 22 episodes. It premiered on September 24 2010 with Exile on Main Street and ended on May 20 2011 with The Man Who Knew Too Much.

Summary

"Freedom is a length of rope that God wants you to hang yourself with."
- Castiel

The sixth season takes place a year after the the fifth season, with Dean living a normal life with Lisa and Ben. When Sam suddenly returns to Dean, strangely emotionless, he is forced to leave his life with Lisa and Ben behind and work with Sam and his extended family, including his ressurected grandpa Samuel Campbell, to capture Alpha monsters for Crowley. Meanwhile Castiel is fighting a Civil War against Raphael in Heaven.

Dean does not trust Samuel and his suspicions are confirmed when he finds him working with demons to go after Purgatory, where creatures go where they die. Dean discovers that Sam doesn't have a soul, which is still in the Cage, so he tries to get Death to retrieve it. He does, but to save Sam the trauma of his memories in the cage at he hands of Lucifer, Death blocks those memories using a mental wall. The second half of the seaason is about the Mother of All, mother of monsters, which leads to events which prove that Castiel was the one that brought Sam back, and he's responsible for the hunt for the Alpha monsters, and the search for Purgatory, all the while he was also working with Crowley. He does this so he can use the souls in purgatory to make himself powerful and defeat Raphael. When the Winchesters try to stop Castiel, he destroys Death's mental wall in Sam, bringing back the memories of the cage, and proceeds with his plan, absorbing all the souls from Purgatory and calling himself God.

Season 7

"You know me. Always happy to bleed for the Winchesters."
- Castiel

The seventh season of Supernatural has 23 episodes. It premiered on September 23 2011 with Meet the New Boss and ended on May 18 2012 with Survival of the Fittest.

Summary

After absorbing souls from Purgatory and declaring himself God, Castiel begins to punish all the bad people in the world, such as a homophobic pastor and corrupt politicians. He discovers that with the souls he also absorbed the Leviathans, ancient creatures God himself locked up, who are attempting to escape from Castiel's vessel. Castiel sees he's not going to last much longer and goes to Sam and Dean for help. They return the souls to Purgatory but the Leviathans cling on to Castiel, then they seemingly kill Castiel and scatter all over the world.

They learn that the Leviathans' weakness is Sodium Borate (also known as Borax) and they can be killed with it, though it doesn't do much to the leader Dick Roman. After Dick kills Bobby, Dean becomes obsessed with taking the Leviathans down and learns of a facility to cure cancer they have. The ghost of Bobby confirms that while they are curing cancer, they are doing it to turn humanity into the perfect food source for them. It turns out Castiel was not dead, but had amnesia, after remembering who he was, he takes on Sam's Hell trauma, which turns him kind of crazy but he does still function. They also meet Kevin, the new prophet, Kevin can read the word of God and helps Sam and Dean decipher it to stop the Leviathans. With the help of Castiel and Kevin, the brothers learn that the only way to kill the Leviathans is with the "bone of a righteous mortal washed in the three bloods of the fallen". Eventually, Dean and Castiel kill Dick but are blasted into Purgatory together as a result, while Sam is left alone to deal with Crowley, who plans to rise to power now that the Leviathans are gone.
